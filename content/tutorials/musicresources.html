arsentical’s music resources document

- I've written this mostly for those who want to use LMMS (hence the specific notes), but almost all of it is applicable to any DAW.
- it is organized to the best of my ability, though that is a low bar. things are often listed in random or no order.
- everything in this document should be either free or have a free version.
- time always marches on, and nothing here will stay accurate forever.

DAWS

you'll need a program to make your music in. here are some good free ones to start, but do your own research to find what works best for you
- everything here should have *full* functionality (no demos)
- finally, if you're on a mac, just use garageband to start. justice did it, you can too

  • LMMS (recommended for this document) https://lmms.io/
  • Cakewalk https://www.bandlab.com/products/cakewalk
  • SoundBridge https://soundbridge.io/
  • Waveform Free (?) https://www.tracktion.com/products/waveform-free
  • *Ardour https://ardour.org/
  • *Zrythm https://www.zrythm.org/
  • OpenMPT https://openmpt.org/
  • Radium http://users.notam02.no/~kjetism/radium/
  • Stargate https://github.com/stargateaudio/stargate

*free if built from source code

INSTRUMENT PLUGINS

synthesizers are pretty much the backbone of electronic music. not all of these may be synths, but they are all useful.

  • ARTIFAKE https://sites.google.com/site/artifakelabs/
  • AMPLE SOUND 1 https://www.amplesound.net/en/index.asp
  • IK MULTIMEDIA 2 https://www.ikmultimedia.com/demodownload/
  • VITAL https://vital.audio/
  • TAL SOFTWARE https://tal-software.com/products
  • YMCK (MAGICAL 8BIT) https://ymck.net/app/magical-8bit-plug-en
  • PLUGINS4FREE https://plugins4free.com/instruments/
  • SYNTH1 https://www.kvraudio.com/product/synth1-by-daichi-laboratory-ichiro-toda
  • KVR https://www.kvraudio.com/plugins/the-newest-plugins/cheapest
  • ZYNADDSUBFX 3 https://zynaddsubfx.sourceforge.io/
  • U-HE https://u-he.com/products/
  • DISCODSP https://www.discodsp.com/
  • SURGE SYNTH 4 https://surge-synthesizer.github.io/

1 has some good free plugins, but you’ll have to look around a bit
2 IK requires a lot of hoop-jumping, and it only allows for free demos usually.
3 Zyn is in LMMS by default, and has free “classic” versions which have a pretty bad ui (comparable to synth1). the new version costs $45
4 VST3 only. will not work with LMMS as of writing

INSTRUMENT SAMPLERS

you will need a sampler to play from instrument sample packs most of the time. some good ones are below- I recommend decent sampler or LABS. note that each is compatible only with some packs. kontakt is most popular with the most packs, and LABS has its own proprietary (free) community within the application

  • KONTAKT PLAYER https://www.native-instruments.com/en/products/komplete/samplers/kontkt-6-player/free-download/
  • DECENT SAMPLER https://www.decentsamples.com/product/decent-sampler-plugin/
  • SPITFIRE LABS https://labs.spitfireaudio.com/
  • SFORZANDO* https://www.plogue.com/products/sforzando.html
  • JUICY SF PLUGIN* https://github.com/Birch-san/juicysfplugin

*soundfont players already exist in most DAWs by default; LMMS has one, FL has the fruity sf player, and so on. chances are you won’t need this, but it’s the best I can find if you do
